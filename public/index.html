Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling our helpline at 73750-21000 or by clicking the 'Book Appointment' button on our website.

Do you offer emergency services 24/7?

Yes, our emergency care and pharmacy services are open 24x7 to handle all medical emergencies.

Are cashless insurance facilities available?

Yes, we tie up with major health insurance providers to offer smooth cashless hospitalization facilities.

What is included in the Free Mega Health Camp?

The camp includes Free OPD, BP check, Sugar test, Nebulization, and a flat 50% discount on tests like Blood, Urine, X-Ray, Ultrasound, and 2D Echo.
