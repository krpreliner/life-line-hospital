Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling our helpline at 73750-21000 or by clicking the 'Book Appointment' button on our website.

Do you offer emergency services 24/7?

Yes, our emergency care, ICU, and pharmacy services are open 24x7 to handle all critical medical emergencies.

Are cashless insurance facilities available?

Yes, we tie up with all major health insurance providers (TPA) to offer smooth and hassle-free cashless hospitalization.

Do you have an in-house laboratory?

Absolutely. We have a fully equipped, automated pathology lab that provides rapid and highly accurate diagnostic results.
